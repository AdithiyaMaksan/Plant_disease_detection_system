About the Project

The Plant Disease Detection project is an AI-powered system designed to help farmers and gardeners identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. By leveraging deep learning techniques, our system can analyze images of plant leaves and identify various diseases affecting different plant species.

Technologies Used

  • Deep Learning (CNN)
  • Flask Web Framework
  • PyTorch
  • Bootstrap

Project Goals

  • Early disease detection
  • Reduce crop losses
  • Provide treatment recommendations
  • Make technology accessible to farmers
